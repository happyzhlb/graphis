Description

R-248 is a sulfuric acid process rutile titanium dioxide, which adopts advanced TINT and particle size controlledtechnique and it is stabilized with zinc salt and enveloped with Multiple elements including zirconium, aluminium,silicon,and phosphorous. It combines excellent properties such as high whiteness, high optical and hiding power,super durability and dispersibility properties.R-248 is an high-quality plastic-specific titanium dioxide.

Applications

It can also be widely used in polyolefins, PVC, ABS , pipe, and leather paste, oil paint.

Typical Property

TiO2 conetent % ≥ 96.6
Brightness % ≥ 96.6
Content of rutile % ≥ 96.6
Tint-reducing power，reynolds number ≥ 96.6
PH value 96.6
Oil absorption g/100个 ≤ 96.6
Specific resistance Ωm ≥ 96.6
Sieve residue（45μm），%（m/m）≤ 96.6
Dispersibility，Hetman ≥ 96.6
Volatile at 105 C，%≤ 96.6
